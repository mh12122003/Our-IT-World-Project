Pantene Anti Dandruff Shampoo

Helps you achieve smooth hair and a dandruff free scalp.

About this product

This anti dandruff shampoo contains formula made up of ZPT and Keratin, which help to:
Get rid of flaky scalp and instead makes it clean and fresh while also nourishing it
Dispose intense dandruff and detangle hair easily
Provide hair and scalp nourishment as it consists of active components that seep deeply in your scalp
This shampoo contains Pro-Vitamin Formula with Japanese Rice Oil Essence. These ingredients help to give your hair the ideal softness whilst also maintaining the protein levels in your hair. In addition, it also helps to improve your hair quality to give you long, satiny and smooth hair.

Ingredients

Water, Sodium Lauryl Sulfate, Sodium Laureth Sulfate, Sodium Chloride, Cocamidopropyl Betaine, Dimethicone, Glycol Distearate, Fragrance, Zinc Pyrithione, Sodium Citrate, Cocamide MEA, Sodium Xylenesulfonate, Sodium Benzoate, Guar Hydroxypropyltrimonium Chloride, Citric Acid, Benzyl Alcohol, Panthenol, Panthenyl Ethyl Ether, Methylchloroisothiazolinone, Methylisothiazolinone

How to use

Gently massage onto wet scalp and hair.
Rinse off thoroughly.
