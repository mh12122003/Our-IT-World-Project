Our Story

Have you ever feel annoyed using products with paper labels? You want to read the information about those products but end up seeing nothing because the ink is blurred out? Calm down, because we are here for you. Noticing such difficulty, we come up with the idea of replacing those paper labels with tiny but effective QR Codes. From now on, all you need to do is scan the QR Code on the product, and this website will provide you,not only information, but other powerful features.
